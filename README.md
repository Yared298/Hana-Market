Hana Market Website

This repository contains the code for a single-page informational website for Hana Market, a family-run Japanese grocery store located on 17th Street NW in Washington, DC.

What is this site for?

The purpose of this website is to provide a digital storefront for the physical market. It acts as a quick-reference guide for neighborhood locals and visitors who want to learn about the shop, check operational details, or see what is in stock before making a trip.

Key Sections Included

The website is structured into sections that cover everything a customer needs to know:

The Story: An introduction to Hana Market, highlighting its welcoming atmosphere, affordable prices, and its role as a neighborhood fixture for authentic Japanese goods.

What We Carry: A breakdown of the inventory, showcasing market staples such as fresh onigiri (rice balls) made daily in-store, produce, tofu, imported Japanese snacks, pantry essentials, and sashimi-grade fish.

Onigiri Spotlight: A reminder advising customers that their popular handmade rice balls sell out quickly and are usually gone by mid-afternoon.

Visit Us: Clear operating hours (11 AM to 8 PM daily) alongside direct links to Google Maps for quick navigation.

Neighborhood Reviews: A collection of real feedback from local shoppers highlighting the store's accessibility, hospitality, and convenience for residents in DC.
